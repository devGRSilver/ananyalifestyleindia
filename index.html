Ananya Lifestyle India Pvt. Ltd. is a forward-thinking, multi-vertical corporate conglomerate headquartered in India. Founded and led by visionary entrepreneur Rahul Goyal, the group has grown from a single bold idea into a diversified ecosystem of premium businesses.

From handcrafted silver jewelry that reaches international markets, to AI-powered astrology consulting that touches millions of lives — Ananya Lifestyle stands at the intersection of tradition and technology, culture and commerce.

Founder & CEO

Rahul Goyal

Entrepreneur · Innovator · Brand Builder

Rahul Goyal's entrepreneurial journey is a testament to vision, resilience, and relentless ambition. Beginning his path at the age of just 18, he dove headfirst into the world of business — a bold decision that would eventually shape an entire corporate ecosystem.

With over 17 years of hands-on experience across diverse industries, Rahul has built, scaled, and led businesses that span jewelry, astrology, information technology, and commercial services. His leadership style is defined by a rare blend of traditional Indian business acumen and forward-thinking modern strategy.

As the founder of GR Silver Designs and RashiYog, Rahul has demonstrated an ability to identify niche opportunities and transform them into recognized national and international brands. His global outlook and entrepreneurial spirit continue to drive Ananya Lifestyle's expansion into new verticals.
